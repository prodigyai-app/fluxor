Fluxor

QR & link maker

Turn your live site address into a scannable QR code — generated on your device, nothing uploaded.

dots / background

Scan it with any phone camera to open your site. Test it before printing or sharing.

  1. Your GitHub Pages address already works as-is — paste it above to make the QR code.
  2. For something shorter to say or type, make a free short link at a redirect service such as TinyURL or is.gd: paste your Pages URL, and you can set a custom ending like tinyurl.com/my-revision.
  3. Paste that short link back in the box above to put it inside the QR code instead.
  4. Tip: a custom domain (in your repo's Settings → Pages) gives the cleanest address of all, e.g. revise.yoursite.com.

All generation happens locally in your browser. Works offline.
